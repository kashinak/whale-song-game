Whale Song

Memory Game

Game Instructions

  • Wait for the computer to play a sequence of highlighted photos begining with one photo.
  • Repeat the game sequence by pressing on corresponding photos.
  • After each round the computer will generate the same sequence, adding one additional photo click.
  • You win after 20 rounds!
  • Game over when you press a photo out of game sequence.

Play the Game! Good Luck!
