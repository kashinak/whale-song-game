Whale Song

Memory Game

Wait for the computer to play a sequence of highlighted photos begining with one photo. Repeat the game sequence by pressing on each corresponding photo. After each round the computer will generate the same sequence, adding one addtional photo click. You win after 20 rounds! Game over when you press a photo out of game sequence.
